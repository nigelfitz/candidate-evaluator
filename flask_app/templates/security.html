Security & Data Privacy

We understand that candidate data is sensitive. We treat security as our highest priority.

We Do Not Train on Your Data

Your resumes and job descriptions are used exclusively for your analyses. We do not use your private data to train public AI models or share it with third parties.

Industry-Standard Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+ and at rest using AES-256 encryption. Your information is stored securely on our PostgreSQL infrastructure.

GDPR Mindset

We follow strict data minimization principles. You have full control to permanently delete your analysis history and candidate data at any time from your Job History page. When you delete an analysis, all associated candidate files and results are permanently removed from our servers. Your transaction history is preserved for accounting purposes, but all candidate-specific data is erased.
